VNVBooks - Bank & Credit Card Transactions Upload Instructions

Format Requirements:
Your CSV file must contain exactly 4 columns in the following order:
1. Date (YYYY-MM-DD or MM/DD/YYYY)
2. Check No (Optional reference number. Leave blank if none)
3. Description (Transaction details / Payee)
4. Amount (Numeric value, no currency symbols)

Understanding Positive and Negative Amounts (CRITICAL):

For BANK ACCOUNTS (Checking, Savings):
- POSITIVE Amount (+): Represents a DEPOSIT (Money coming in).
- NEGATIVE Amount (-): Represents a WITHDRAWAL (Money going out).

For CREDIT CARDS:
- POSITIVE Amount (+): Represents a CHARGE (You bought something, increasing your debt).
- NEGATIVE Amount (-): Represents a CREDIT/PAYMENT (You paid the card off, or got a refund).
